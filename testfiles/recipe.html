Bacon Buldak Pasta 🥓

Serves 6
1×
30 mins One-pot REALLY GOOD

Ingredients

  • 2 oz pasta (shape of choice)
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 slices bacon (chopped)
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp buldak hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup onion (diced)
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

Fry the bacon

In a pan, fry your chopped bacon until all the fat has been rendered out. Then, remove the bacon from the pan and pour out the fat.

Fry the onion and garlic

In the same pan, pour in garlic and onion. Stir fry until you feel like stopping lol doesn't really matter🤣

Pasta time!

Pour pasta into the same pan and fill with water until pasta is covered.

Spicy buldak! (Optional if don't want spice)

Add the buldak into the pasta and let it boil together! Pasta absorb all the buldak!!

Waiting for the pasta to cook!

Leave the pan uncovered and monitor the pasta. After a few minutes, pour the bacon back into the pasta until the pasta is cooked (If you want crispy bacon, you can save some to sprinkle in later).

Parm and black pepper time!

As the water evaporates most of the way, sprinkle the black pepper and add the parmesan into the pasta.

Saucing it up

Once the pasta is dry enough (up to you but I like a little liquid), turn off heat. With heat off, add cottage cheese into the pasta, mix mix mix, and serve! If you saved some bacon, sprinkle on top now!
